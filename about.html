About Me

Hello! My name is Michael and I am 26 years old. I grew up in Colorado my whole life and have always had a love for cats. My love for cats began when I was young with my first cat Tiger (pictured below is the both of us). Now I have two cats named Daisy and Jasmine who are sisters. Daisy is the white cat named after the flower and Jasmine is named after the princess Jasmine from Disney's Aladin. When I was in elementary school I would always read any books I could find on big cats of the world. That is what inspired me to create this website and hopfully it is able to inspire somebody else to pursue research on these majestic animals.
