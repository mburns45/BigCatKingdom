About The Tiger

Tigers can be found in China and Eastern Russia, they like to thrive in tropical forests. A full grown tiger can reach lengths of 10 ft long and up to 660 lbs. This makes the tiger one of the biggest cats in the world. You would think that tigers being orange would make them stand out in the jungle but that is the opposite. They prey on deer and the deer's eyes don't allow them to see orange making the tiger very difficult to spot. There are approximately 5,500 tigers alive in the wild.

Quick Facts

  • Tigers stripes are as unique as human fingerprints
  • They hunt alone at night
  • Tigers cannot purr
  • White tigers grow faster than an orange tiger
